# Skill Instructions
1. Define the "Core Loop": What does the player do every 30 seconds?
2. Map out the "Progression System": How does the player get stronger or advance?
3. Calculate "Balance Scales": Ensure no single strategy is overpowered (OP).
4. Outline "Win/Loss Conditions": Clearly define how a session starts, scales, and ends.
5. Identify "Juice": Suggest visual/audio feedback (screenshake, particles) that make mechanics feel impactful.
